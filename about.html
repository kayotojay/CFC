Who We Are

More Than a Meal —

Crispy Fried Chicken started as a dream shared between two childhood friends who believed that fried chicken could be elevated into a fine dining art form. Today, we are a beloved community staple — a destination for families, celebrations, and anyone who refuses to compromise on flavor.

Every piece of chicken that leaves our kitchen carries with it decades of refined technique, hand-sourced ingredients, and an uncompromising love for the craft. We are not a fast food joint. We are a restaurant with soul.

Our Journey

A Legacy Built
One Bite at a Time

2009

Where It All Began

Jason and Mickayla opened the first CFC location on Flavor Street with just twelve tables, a secondhand fryer, and a family recipe passed down three generations. The first week, they sold out every single night.

2012

The Signature Brine

After three years of experimentation, our master chef perfected the 24-hour buttermilk brine technique — now the cornerstone of every CFC recipe. That same year, we won our first City's Best Fried Chicken award.

2015

Fine Dining Reinvented

We launched our full fine dining experience — white tablecloths, curated wine pairings, and a tasting menu built around fried chicken as the star. Critics called it "the boldest restaurant concept in the city."

2018

The Event Venue Opens

We converted our upstairs space into a full private events venue, hosting weddings, corporate dinners, birthday galas, and everything in between. Our first private event was a 200-person wedding — flawlessly executed.

2021

Community Through Crisis

During the pandemic years, CFC pivoted to feed frontline workers and partnered with local shelters to donate over 40,000 meals. We emerged stronger, more connected to our neighborhood than ever.

2024

Expanding the Legacy

CFC opened its second and third locations, bringing our signature flavor to two new neighborhoods. Both sold out reservations within 48 hours of announcing. The legacy keeps growing.

Our Mission

To Make Every Guest
Feel Like Family

Our mission is simple: serve the most exceptional fried chicken experience on earth, in an environment where every guest feels celebrated. We believe great food is a form of love, and we pour that love into every dish, every service, every event.

Craft

We never cut corners. Every piece is hand-seasoned, slow-brined, and cooked to order. Perfection isn't a goal — it's the standard.

Community

We are embedded in the neighborhoods we serve. From local sourcing to charity partnerships, CFC gives back as much as it receives.

Innovation

We respect tradition, but we are never afraid to experiment. Our seasonal menus push boundaries while honoring the classics that made us famous.

Hospitality

Service is not what we do — it's who we are. Every guest receives the warmth and attention of a meal in someone's home.

Sustainability

We source locally, compost kitchen waste, and use biodegradable packaging. Delicious food should never come at the planet's expense.

Excellence

Twelve industry awards and counting. We celebrate each recognition not as a finish line, but as motivation to raise the bar even higher.
